tests.sic.rnqt.nbin package: implements exact and/or heuristic algorithms for the global optimization of long-run expected average costs of a single-echelon inventory control system operating a periodic review control policy (R,nQ,T) facing stochastic demands that are distributed according to the Negative Binomial distribution (a compound Poisson distribution where customers arrive at intervals that are spaced according to the Poisson distribution, but their order quantities are logarithmically distributed instead of being always single unit demands) in a continuous time cost-accounting setting.

Package Specification

The formulae for the average cost of such a system are taken from the classic IE textbook of Hadley and Whitin, "Analysis of Inventory Systems", 1963. It is worth noting here that numerical integration is involved in the computation of the back-order costs as closed form formulae are not readily available.
