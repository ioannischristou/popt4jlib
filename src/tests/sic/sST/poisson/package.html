tests.sic.sST.poisson package: implements exact and/or heuristic algorithms for the global optimization of long-run expected average costs of a single-echelon inventory control system operating a periodic review control policy (s,S,T) facing Poisson distributed demands in a continuous time cost-accounting setting.

Package Specification

The formulae for the average cost of such a system are taken from the classic IE textbook of Hadley and Whitin, "Analysis of Inventory Systems", 1963.
