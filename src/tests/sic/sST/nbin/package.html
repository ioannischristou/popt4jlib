tests.sic.sST.nbin package: implements exact and heuristic algorithms for the global optimization of long-run exact average costs of a single-echelon inventory control system operating a periodic review control policy (s,S,T) facing sporadic demands modeled as a compound Poisson process, and in particular the Negative Binomial process with Poisson arrivals and Logarithmic distribution of the quantities asked, with parameters λ and pl in a continuous time cost-accounting setting.

Package Specification

The formulae for the cost of such a system are computed based on the generic formulae in Hadley and Whitin (1963): Analysis of Inventory Systems.
